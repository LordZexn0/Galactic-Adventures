Basic Information

The Moon Tour package offered by Galactic Adventures is designed to take you on a once-in-a-lifetime journey to the edge of space. The Lunar Tour will last approximately 10 days, during which you will have the chance to experience the breathtaking beauty of our planet from a unique perspective.

Our vehicle will reach an altitude of around Geostationary orbit (GEO), 22,236 miles, above the Earth's surface, providing you with stunning views of the Earth and the surrounding cosmos. You will experience weightlessness and have the chance to float freely within the vehicle, taking in the incredible sights around you. As well as orbit the whole Earth.

Vehicles

At Galactic Adventures, we use only the most advanced and reliable vehicles for our space tourism trips. While our other packages, including planetary orbit, use a rocket ship design, the lunar exploration packages include a more advanced spacecraft designed exclusively for lunar travel.

Our LM-120 space shuttle is equipped with the latest in spaceflight technology, including state-of-the-art life support systems, communication equipment, and navigation systems. The interior of the ship is designed to provide maximum comfort, with spacious cabins, private sleeping quarters, and ample storage space. It is specifically designed to accommodate travelers who want to go to the moon and even potentially to mars.

The engines of our rocket-ship are powered by cutting-edge propulsion technology, designed to deliver maximum thrust and efficiency during all stages of flight. Our engines are engineered to withstand the extreme conditions of space travel and provide the necessary power to reach our destination and return safely to Earth.

Continuing with the LM series, a feature of Lunar probes as well as rovers will accompany you on your galactic voyage. This includes the LM-870 automated probe which gives you live access to the lunar surface with a virtual interaction menu right from the comfort of the lunar base. The LM-2180 lunar rover is specially designed to roam the lunar surface. With its cutting-edge tire technology, it can handle the most rigid of lunar craters and hike the tallest of lunar peaks.

The last part of the LM series is an escape pod included in the space shuttle for safety escape. This pod seats up to 5 individuals and provides basic essentials and enough oxygen for a 2 day trip back to earth. To keep bulk down and allow this craft to be compact, it does not include all the comforts of a normal craft but enough to allow any visitors to survive and safely arrive back to earth.

Launch

The launch of the Lunar package offered by Galactic Adventures is a highly choreographed event that requires the coordination of many different teams and systems. Our experienced launch team works closely with organizations such as JPL and Boeing to ensure a smooth and safe launch. Going to the moon is no easy task, and we look forward to helping you get there in the best way possible

On launch day, the space shuttle will be fueled and undergo final checks before being transported to the launch pad. The launch is a once-in-a-lifetime experience, and you will never forget the thrill of leaving the Earth behind and venturing on your way to the moon and beyond as the shuttle, with all its might, takes you away from the night sky and to the moon.

Recovery

The recovery process for Galactic Adventures' Lunar exploratory package is an equally important aspect of your space journey. Our experienced recovery team works closely with JPL and Boeing to ensure a safe and smooth return to Earth.

After 10 days of travel on the moon, the vehicle will begin its descent back to the Earth Which will roughly take about 2 days. The engines will engage to slow the vehicle and guide it back to a predetermined landing site. Throughout the descent, the recovery team will closely monitor the vehicle and make any necessary adjustments to ensure a safe landing. In case of an emergency, the escape pods will be on standby.

Since the shuttle carries large amounts of cargo, using the space shuttle design allows us to easily recover your shuttle as it has a designated runway for it to land on.

Upon landing, the vehicle will be recovered and the astronauts will undergo medical evaluations to ensure they are in good health after their journey. They will also undergo debriefing to discuss their experiences and provide valuable feedback to help improve future missions.

The recovery process marks the end of an incredible adventure, but the memories and experiences gained from your journey will last a lifetime. Galactic Adventures is committed to ensuring a safe and memorable experience for all of our customers, and the recovery process is just one part of that commitment.
